Getting Started

Up and running
in minutes

Three simple steps to connect your employee portal to your phone.

1

Download the app

Download the APK file and install it on your Android device. Allow installation from unknown sources if prompted.

2

Connect your site

Enter your company's WordPress site URL that's running WP-ERP. The app will validate the connection automatically.

3

Log in & go

Sign in with your employee credentials and start managing your work life on the go. It's that simple.
